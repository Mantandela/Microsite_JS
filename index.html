FAQs & Quick Help

Q1: How do I get my student ID?

A: Visit the library groundfloor for biometrics with your admission letter, the proceed to the registrars office to collect your school id

Q2: Where can I photocopy or print documents?

A: Printing and photocopying services are available in the library and the student center.

Q3: Who do I talk to if I miss a class?

A: Notify your course instructor via email and consult the Blackboard portal for missed materials.

Q4: What happens if I lose a library book?

A: Report immediately to the library. You may need to replace the book or pay a fine depending on library policy.

Q5: How do I know when to register for classes?

A: Registration periods and deadlines are always sent to your USIU student email. Check regularly to avoid missing them.

Q6: Where can I find past exam papers?

A: They are available in the eLibrary and sometimes in course-specific sections in Blackboard.
